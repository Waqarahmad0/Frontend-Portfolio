WHO WE ARE

Since 1995 Constellation Software Inc is acquiring and transforming software enterprises globally and catering to the diverse needs of organizations and institutions from every niche. From being a rapidly growing corporation of vertical market software (VMS) companies to sustaining a robust position on the Toronto Stock Exchange in 2006, Constellation has maintained annual revenues of over $3 billion with its existence in more than 100 countries.

As a subsidiary of CSI, Contour Software serves as a dedicated Global Centre, currently housing employees and teams for more than 150 Divisional and Corporate departments. Contour employees are key players in implementing, supporting, extending, enhancing, and renewing enterprise systems that run thousands of medium and large businesses, as well as public institutions, globally!
